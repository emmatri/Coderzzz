Nutrition =======

Studies show that 46% of adults will experience stress or anxiety in their lifetime. A rather surprising set of statistics reveals that some of the most stressed members of society are students and young adults falling within the 18-24 bracket. Very worryingly, 2018 studies demonstrate they struggle with stress for over 12 days of every month on average and are the age group most likely to worry about its impact on their health. Stress and anxiety can have a negative impact on our bodies and serenity is here to help make small steps to a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Serenity is a calm haven of hints and tips to reduce stress and boost mood. Please explore our pages to find out more about how nutrition and exercise can help on your journey to a healthier and calmer you.
